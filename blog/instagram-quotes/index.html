How to Use Quotes for Instagram with Templates

Now that we’ve covered some of the best ways to make the most of Instagram quotes, let’s look at how you can make your quotes fit with your aesthetic.

Remember, you can’t just post any old font on a white background – unless that suits the image you’re trying to create for your company. Instead, you may need a template that you can access whenever you want to publish a spiritual message or moving quote.

Fortunately, there are plenty of tools that can help you get the most out of your quotes for Instagram. Here are just some of our favorite options.

1.¬†¬†¬†¬† Canva

Canva is a go-to tool for Instagram users. With this fantastic image editing software, you can create templates for everything from Instagram Highlights to Instagram Stories and more. As an easy-to-use offering intended to suit businesses of any size or background, Canva is excellent whether you’re a huge corporation or a small influencer looking to spruce up your Instagram account.

With Canva, you can design unique backgrounds for your Instagram quotes, explore different kinds of fonts to suit your individual style, and even apply filters. There’s even the option to add custom dimensions if you want to. Don’t forget to add your Instagram username or branded hashtag to your post to remind people that the image came from you.

2.¬†¬†¬†¬† ReciteThis

ReciteThis is another simple and straightforward browser-based tool that Instagram users can access to design their quotes for Instagram. All you need to do to create a new post is select one of the pre-made background themes from the ReciteThis collection, then press “Create.” From that point, you’ll have the option to upload your image to a range of social networks or download it to post to Instagram. Unfortunately, there’s no direct Instagram upload option yet.

The only major downside to this tool is that there is a watermark in the bottom of the image. However, since you’re downloading your content before you post it, you can always crop that watermark out if you want to.

3.¬†¬†¬†¬† FaceGarage

Here’s another popular Instagram application that helps businesses and individuals to design custom posts for Instagram. You can either use one of the stock background images included with the app to create your Instagram quote or upload a picture of your own. Once you’ve got your background sorted, type in your quote, and adjust the formatting.

FaceGarage allows users to adjust numerous components of their quotes, including the text size, font style, and color. Once your image is generated, you’ll be able to download it (without a watermark). That means that there’s no editing necessary before you post your new material straight to Insta.

4.¬†¬†¬†¬† InstaQuote

InstaQuote is a free app that offers plenty of great options to people who want to design and post fantastic quotes for Instagram. With this tool, you can either use one of the existing photos on InstaQuote’s database or your own images to begin designing your posts. Additionally, you’ll have the option to automatically upload your image to Instagram when you’re down editing. This is a massive benefit for people who are tired of downloading an image from another service and then having to upload it separately.

The biggest issue with this tool is that you will need to upgrade to the premium option if you want the broadest range of editing tools and fonts. However, the same could be said for a lot of free tools available with Instagram.

Using Quotes for Instagram in Your Social Campaign

It’s easy to overlook Instagram quotes as a way to fill the gap in your content strategy when you’ve run out of other, more creative ideas. However, there’s a lot more to quotes for Instagram than you might think. The truth is that if you use them correctly, the right messages, overlaid with the right filters and colors, can make a significant impact on your target audience.

With carefully planned Instagram quotes, you’ll be able to introduce your audience to the unique personality of the people behind your brand. This gives you a fantastic opportunity to build meaningful relationships with your target audience. What’s more, Instagram quotes and captions are also an excellent chance to show off your testimonials, highlight messages for influencers and more.
